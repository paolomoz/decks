What the shape tells us

Provisioning is solved. The biggest cliff is between "logged in" and "actually started building." That's where most users feel the gravity of their existing process — review cycles, content lock-in, brand sign-off, legal.

Q2 hypothesis: Activation drops because the first session has no obvious "win" inside 15 minutes. Fix → opinionated onboarding that ships one live page in the first 15 minutes, before any project structure.
