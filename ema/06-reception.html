Implementation time was never the bottleneck. Reviews, sign-offs, legal, content lock, marketing calendar — that's where the year goes. EMA gives back weeks. The customer needs us to give back months.

Reason · I

The review tax.

Every page touched by AI gets re-reviewed by humans afraid to put their name on output they didn't author. The savings on generation are eaten by the cost of trust.

  • Brand sign-off
  • Legal & reg
  • Visual fidelity
  • Accessibility

Reason · II

The calendar tax.

Migration is rarely the critical path. Customers schedule go-lives around campaigns, board cycles, and risk windows. A 4-week migration in a 15-month program changes nothing about the date.

  • Marketing cal.
  • SI / agency contracts
  • Procurement
  • "Quiet quarters"
